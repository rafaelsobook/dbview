Game Overview

Inspired by reincarnation anime series, A game where you got reincarnated and left with responsibility to explore, travel and face many foe. A game where you risk and die only once. With many places to explore and add recources to gather and craft.

The Game does not support automatic quests that you will sit and watch as your character do everything, The game is only for real adventurers who loves risk and real adventure

Choose A Race

In this game there are different types of races, There is elves who are fast and reliable with their swords, A demon who are enriched with mana and magic, Lastly Divines who are currently discovered by their use of holy magic. Each races have their own strengths. By default player starts with human race. You can change your race by using a forbidden scroll.

Game Overview

Being reincarnated in a different world where magic exist. Awaken to a fresh start in a fantasy realm, where the Gods have chosen you to be their hero. The villagers where you are reborn has many things to offer. They lead you to your adventurous life. They support you with instructions and starting tools. With their guidelines you can easily prevent dying from the game. A person named Nick is one of the descendants that will help you in this journey. You will embark in this new environment, Gain strength, learn new skills and conquer dungeons. As you go further in this new realm, You will face many powerful foes. Many monsters are causing chaos now that there is no God that can stop them.

The Gods have chosen you to defeat this monster and defeat the traitor that trap them into the void. A certain someone that is powerful enough to shake the balance of this world. defeating this person can help the Gods rebuild their stolen strength so they can escape from the void. It is up to the young heroes who has been reborn in this world. Will they Succeed Or Will They Die ...
